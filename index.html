About

From site execution to smart-building research

I'm a civil engineering graduate currently working as a Diploma Engineer Trainee at Maithan Steel & Power Limited, while pursuing my B.Tech in Civil Engineering.

My interest in construction goes beyond execution — during my diploma at Al-Kabir Polytechnic, Jamshedpur, I developed Lumina Concrete, a working prototype demonstrating how embedded light-transmitting elements let daylight pass through concrete. That project was recognized at NIT Jamshedpur and became the foundation for my ongoing venture, LumiWall AI.

My goal is to combine civil engineering fundamentals with modern technology to make construction smarter, more energy-efficient, and more human-centered.
